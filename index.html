How to play

  • Pick one team each gameweek before the deadline (kickoff of the first match). Change it as often as you like until then.
  • A win scores 1 point; draws and losses score nothing.
  • No repeats: once you've used a team it's locked until the reshuffle at gameweek 20 — then every team frees up again.
  • Unique-pick bonus: if you're the only one to back a team that week, your points for it are doubled.
  • Everyone's picks stay hidden from each other until the gameweek locks.
  • If your team's match is postponed (a "forfeit"), that week scores 0 but the team isn't used up — you can pick it again.

Chips

One chip per gameweek, each usable once per half-season (GW1–19 and GW20+). Chips stack with the unique-pick bonus.
